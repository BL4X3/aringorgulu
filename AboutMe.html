Journey

   My journey in the world of design and development began in Turkey, where I initially pursued a degree in Industrial Design at Yeditepe University. However, I soon realized that my true passion lay elsewhere. My curiosity and enthusiasm for the game industry led me to discover the Polish-Japanese Academy of Information Technology in Poland. I made the bold decision to move and immerse myself in a new environment to follow my dreams.

   Currently, I am a third-year student, deeply engaged in the study of game development and design. My passion for the game industry drives my academic and personal projects, and I aspire to become a game developer/designer. I am not only proficient in design but also highly interested in the technical aspects of development, constantly learning and growing in this field.

   One of my greatest strengths is my ability to learn quickly and adapt to new challenges. I thrive when faced with challenges and always push myself when things are easy. If I'm not learning new things, I find it difficult to stay focused. This flexibility allows me to thrive in various roles, whether as a leader or a team player, though i have leadership skills and i would like to strive for more and develop all the time.
